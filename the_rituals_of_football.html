 THE RITUAL OF FOOTBALL

Football acts like a ritual on many levels (see thesis). This project shows the top 10 elements of the ritual in a visual way. The first five are the essential physical parts, without those there's no football-ritual. The field is the central focus and the most fundamental.After the first five elements there's the space in which the ritual takes places. The stadium was the permanent place of the ritual, but the digital media brought a shift in the space of the ritual. Nowadays you can take part of the ritual everywhere there's access to a screen. A Transnational space where the participants enter a common time-zone for 90 minutes long and where it is experienced as live. Sharing emotions and experiences still happen collectively and it is still a physical part of the ritual. However, the sharing of experiences has expanded to the online-world, so collectivity can now also be experienced while you are physically alone. The screen has become the binding center. The physical public space is now an online public space.
